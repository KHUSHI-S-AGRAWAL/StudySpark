# 📚 StudySpark Demo Script

This script is designed for a video demonstration or voiceover walkthrough of the StudySpark application.

---

### **Scene 1: The Landing Page (0:00 - 0:15)**
**Visual:** Show the beautiful dark slate and sky-blue landing page. Move the mouse around to show the dynamic hover effects and the massive, glowing `StudySpark` logo. 
**Voiceover:** 
*"Welcome to StudySpark—your ultimate AI-powered study assistant. We built StudySpark with a stunning, premium dark-mode interface that doesn’t just look good, but makes studying an immersive experience."*

**Action:** Click the bright blue **"🚀 Let's Get Started"** button.

---

### **Scene 2: Document Upload (0:15 - 0:30)**
**Visual:** Show the 'Upload Documents' screen. Drag and drop a sample PDF (like a lecture slide or past exam paper) into the uploader. 
**Voiceover:** 
*"It all starts with your materials. You can seamlessly drag and drop your lecture notes, past papers, or even image-based notes. We process everything in parallel behind the scenes to give you instant, lightning-fast context."*

**Action:** Click **"Process Documents"**. Wait for the spinner to finish.

---

### **Scene 3: The Feature Grid (0:30 - 0:45)**
**Visual:** Show the main feature grid. Slowly hover over the different cards ("Analytics Dashboard", "Smart Tutor", "Quiz", etc.) to demonstrate the glowing spotlight tracking animation.
**Voiceover:** 
*"Once your documents are analyzed, you're presented with your study dashboard. Notice the gorgeous interactive spotlight effects as you hover over your study tools. Let's dive into some of the core features."*

---

### **Scene 4: Analytics Dashboard (0:45 - 1:00)**
**Action:** Click **"📊 Analytics Dashboard"**.
**Visual:** Show the AI Pattern Analytics page. Scroll through the generated charts (Topic Frequency, Importance, and Question Types).
**Voiceover:** 
*"Our AI Pattern Analytics engine scans your past papers to figure out exactly what your professors like to test. It automatically generates interactive charts showing the most frequently asked topics and their importance scores, so you know exactly what to study first."*

---

### **Scene 5: Summary & Important Points (1:00 - 1:15)**
**Action:** Click **"📝 Summary"** and click "Generate Summary". Then go back and click **"🎯 Important Points"**.
**Visual:** Show the generated text. Highlight the Listen and Stop buttons at the top and click **"🔊 Listen"**.
**Voiceover:** 
*"Need a quick refresher? Use the Summary or Important Points features to instantly extract the most critical bullet points and formulas. And with our native Text-to-Speech engine, you can simply hit 'Listen' and let the AI read the notes aloud to you."*

---

### **Scene 6: Study Strategy & Practice Questions (1:15 - 1:30)**
**Action:** Click **"🧠 How to Study"**, then generate the strategy. Go back and click **"💡 Practice Qs"** and generate questions.
**Visual:** Show the AI recommending a study strategy, followed by a list of 5 highly probable practice questions with difficulty levels and solutions.
**Voiceover:** 
*"StudySpark doesn’t just summarize; it guides you. Ask the AI for a custom 'How to Study' strategy based on your material, or instantly generate highly probable practice questions to test your application of the concepts."*

---

### **Scene 7: Smart Tutor & Video Resources (1:30 - 1:45)**
**Action:** Click **"👨‍🏫 Smart Tutor"** and ask a question. Then go back and click **"🎥 Video Resources"**.
**Visual:** Show the chatbot answering a complex question. Then show the Video Resources page suggesting YouTube links.
**Voiceover:** 
*"Stuck on a tough concept? Chat directly with the Smart Tutor—it knows exactly what’s in your documents. If you prefer visual learning, the Video Resources feature will automatically recommend the best YouTube videos tailored to your syllabus."*

---

### **Scene 8: Practice Quiz (1:45 - 2:00)**
**Action:** Click **"❓ Quiz"**, set the slider to 3 questions, and generate. Select answers and submit.
**Visual:** Show the interactive radio buttons and the final score screen.
**Voiceover:** 
*"When you're ready for a real test, our Quiz Engine dynamically generates multiple-choice questions based entirely on your study material. Submit your answers and get instant grading and detailed explanations."*

---

### **Scene 9: Smart Planner & Outro (2:00 - 2:15)**
**Action:** Click **"📅 Smart Planner"**, enter '14' days, and generate.
**Visual:** Show the generated markdown table schedule.
**Voiceover:** 
*"Finally, if you're cramming for an exam, our Smart Planner will build a customized, day-by-day study timetable that prioritizes your weakest topics. StudySpark builds the ultimate strategy for your success. Happy studying!"*
